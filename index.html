About Us

Cast and Craft Ventures is a dynamic construction company dedicated to providing a comprehensive range of services, from initial vision to project completion. Our team of young professionals combines expertise in feasibility studies, environmental stewardship, architectural services, and quantity surveying to offer innovative solutions that meet your needs and budget.

We are passionate about building spaces that are not only functional and aesthetically pleasing but also environmentally responsible. Whether you have a residential, commercial, or industrial project, our goal is to bring your vision to life while promoting sustainability and environmental conservation.

You dream it, we create it.

Our Mission

To craft spaces that integrate functionality, aesthetics, and environmental sustainability.

Our Vision

Home of innovative construction solutions, providing long-lasting, efficient, and environmentally friendly projects.

Our Services

Sustainable Design & Construction

We create functional and beautiful spaces that minimize environmental impact. Our sustainable construction practices focus on eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs to ensure your projects are not only stunning but also responsible.

Impact Assessments & Audits

Our team ensures that every project we handle is both environmentally and socially responsible. Through comprehensive impact assessments and audits, we provide detailed reports that meet all regulatory requirements.

Project Feasibility & Market Surveys

We help you navigate the complexities of your project by conducting in-depth feasibility studies and market surveys. This ensures that your project is viable, financially sound, and aligned with market demands.

Architectural Expertise & Quantity Surveying

From concept to completion, we bring your vision to life with our architectural expertise and cost-effective quantity surveying services. Our designs balance creativity with efficiency, ensuring a seamless building process.

Effluent Discharge Licensing

We guide you through the legalities of your project with ease, ensuring compliance with all environmental regulations, including effluent discharge licensing.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)

We provide extensive expertise in conducting thorough Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs) for development projects, ensuring compliance with the Environmental Management and Coordination Act (EMCA) of Kenyan laws.

Environmental Audits

Our annual audits evaluate the environmental performance of NEMA-approved projects, identifying gaps and recommending corrective actions to ensure compliance and reduce risks.

Water Quality Analysis

We offer water quality assessments, monitoring, and management to help clients meet NEMA standards, ensuring safe and sustainable water resources through testing, analysis, and actionable recommendations.

Consultation and Public Participation

We facilitate public consultation and engagement as part of the Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) process, ensuring that projects align with both environmental and social expectations.

Occupational Health and Safety (OSH)

Our specialized OSH consultancy helps businesses comply with safety regulations, creating safe working environments through robust safety solutions.

Construction Environmental Site Inspections

We conduct environmental inspections on construction sites to ensure regulatory compliance, identify issues, and enforce corrective actions for sustainability.

EDGE Expert (Green Building Practices)

We offer training on EDGE (Excellence in Design for Greater Efficiencies) certification, promoting green building practices that drive sustainability in construction.

Quantity Surveying

Our quantity surveying services provide accurate cost estimation, budget development, and financial oversight to optimize resources and ensure successful project outcomes.

Contracts Management

We offer expert consultancy in contracts management, ensuring all legal and contractual obligations are met for the smooth execution of construction projects.

Cost Analysis and Software Implementation

We utilize software tools like BIM, Bluebeam, and Planswift to deliver accurate cost analysis, enhancing the precision and efficiency of project management.

Architecture

We specialize in modern, sustainable architectural design, using the latest technologies to deliver innovative, functional, and aesthetically driven solutions tailored to client needs.

Feasibility Studies

We provide in-depth project viability analysis, financial projections, risk assessments, and strategic planning to support informed decision-making and optimize project outcomes.

Housing Market Analysis

We provide insights into housing trends, pricing dynamics, market segmentation, and consumer behavior, helping clients understand opportunities and make informed decisions.

Periodic Write-Ups on Housing

We publish timely reports, newsletters, and articles on housing sector developments, regulatory changes, and industry trends, providing actionable insights to help clients stay informed.

Tendering with PPRA

We offer end-to-end support in navigating the tendering process with the Public Procurement Regulatory Authority (PPRA), providing guidance for successful bids and project procurement.
